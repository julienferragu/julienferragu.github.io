## Short note

Experimental analysis becomes much more valuable when it can be monitored. That means surfacing quality checks, failure modes, confidence signals and review paths instead of only returning a final prediction.

## Tooling priorities

- Diagnostics that make noisy inputs visible early.
- Reproducible outputs tied to runs, parameters and metadata.
- Review loops for borderline or low-confidence classifications.
- Validation summaries that help move from prototype to operational use.

## Relevance

This is the bridge between applied ML work and a tool that can actually support decision-making in an experimental setting.
