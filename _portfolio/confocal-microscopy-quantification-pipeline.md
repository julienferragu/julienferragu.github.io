## Focus

At INSERM INMED, I built a 3D confocal microscopy quantification pipeline for cellular marker segmentation, counting and statistical analysis. The goal was to improve robustness and reduce repetitive manual processing for imaging data analysis.

## Pipeline logic

- Segmentation and quantification of cellular markers on microscopy data.
- Counting and statistical analysis integrated into the same workflow.
- Reduced manual repetition by turning an analysis routine into a reproducible pipeline.
- Designed for research-team use rather than ad hoc one-shot scripts.

## Why it matters

This project is shorter than the two main case studies, but it is strong evidence of practical pipeline building on heterogeneous biological data beyond acoustic and behavioural signals.

## Relevance

It demonstrates breadth across biological data modalities and a consistent pattern of turning complex analysis needs into robust, reusable tools.
